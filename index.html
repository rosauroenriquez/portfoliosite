About Me

Hi everyone, I am Rosauro "Ross" Enriquez. I am a US Navy Veteran. I served 8 years in the Navy as a Gas Turbine Electrical Technician. I specialized in operating, maintaining, and troubleshooting all kinds of electrical and electronic components within the Engineering Department. Some of the major systems I worked on include Engine Controllers, Control Consoles, Switchboards, etc.

It's been a goal of mine to be a software engineer. I fell in love with programming back when I was still a college student, and that is why after I finished my military service, I started learning how to code again. Currently, I am enrolled in Microsoft Software and Systems Academy (Cloud Application Development). I've been learning .NET, C#, and all the goodness this environment has to offer. I am aiming for Junior Developer roles and really excited to learn more about software development. I know with my unique skills and experiences in the Navy, plus my creativity in solving problems, I can be an asset to any team or organization.
